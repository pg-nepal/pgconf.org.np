We invite proposals for sponsorships for the event PGCONF Nepal 2025. Benefits and prices of sponsorships are as mentioned below

Platinum Sponsorship

Benefits:

  1. Permission for the Business to use the following endorsement: “Platinum Partner of PostgreSQL Conference Nepal 2025” on the Business' website or in any promotional materials or press releases that the Business sees fit.

  2. Prominent inclusion of the Business' logo and a link to the Business' website on the official conference website (the logo to be provided by Business).

  3. The Business' logo printed on the official PostgreSQL Conference Nepal 2025 attendees t-shirts and certificates (the logo to be provided by Business).

  4. Inclusion of three A4 or Letter sized pages of the Business' promotional material - optionally in a folder without bindings - in the conference packs for registered attendees (the paperwork to be provided by Business).

  5. Two free attendee passes for the conference (not including admission to training sessions), subject to availability (registration required, seats are not reserved until registration is completed).

  6. A desk in the exhibitors area of the conference for the promotion of the Business' products or services.

  7. Inclusion of one or two small Business promotional giveaways in the conference packs for registered attendees, such as a pen, USB memory stick or pin (the giveaway to be provided by Business).

Cost of sponsorship-

(3000 USD)

Up to one platinum sponsor may be accepted, on a first come, first served basis.
