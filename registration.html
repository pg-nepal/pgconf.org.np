Training

Training will be considered as a pre-conference event (9-10 May).

Training Cost

Regular: NRs 10,000.00 (NRs. Ten Thousands only)

Discount: Students can apply for the student discount as well. There will be NRs 2000 waive on the registration charge for the student (limited seats are available).

The cost includes

  1. 2 Days PostgreSQL training session by an industry expert

  2. Travel from Kathmandu pick up point to training venue

  3. Light Breakfast, Lunch, Tea, Coffee and Water

  4. Training Kit

  5. Hands on notes and training materials

  6. T-shirt with PostgreSQL logo etc.

Note: The bank account details for the payment will be shared separately on the email provided by participant.

Main Confernce

The main event of the PostgreSQL Conference 2023 is the conference day (5-6 May).

Conference Cost

For Nepalese participants

  1. Early bird : NRs. 4000.00 First 15 registration

  2. Regular : NRs. 5000.00 Upto 30 participants

For Foreign Participants : USD 100

The cost includes

  1. Pickup from Kathmandu pick up point

  2. Tea/Coffee, Lunch and water at venue

  3. Registration to the main conference and any parallel sessions

  4. 1 Night stay at Dhulikhel where participants can network with each other and international delegates

  5. Gala Dinner at Dhulikhel

  6. Conference kit with T-Shirt and others

Note: The bank account details for the payment will be shared separately on the email provided by participant.
